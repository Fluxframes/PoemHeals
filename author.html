About the Author

I’m Bhawna Jaiswal — the soul behind PoemHeals. A voice that echoes through verses, I’ve transformed emotions into words and words into healing. My poems aren’t just lines, they’re lived feelings wrapped in syllables.

With over 1 million readers and 200+ audiobooks crafted, my journey has been nothing short of magical. Every poem is born from pain, love, hope, and everything in between — meant to be felt, not just read.

PoemHeals isn’t just a project. It’s a refuge — for those who feel deeply, love loudly, and sometimes break silently. You’ll find pieces of your soul here.

Through voice and verse, I invite you to sit with your feelings, to be still, and to know you’re never alone in what you carry.
